Go! Wet Waterpark Grand Wisata Bekasi

Go! Wet Waterpark is a relatively new waterpark located in Grand Wisata City, Bekasi. It's one of the largest waterpark in Bekasi. It has so many slides, pool, and many more. The food stall and the food court is also great in the place. This waterpark is an excellent choice to spend your weekend with your family.

Summarecon Mall Bekasi

Anyone in Bekasi who love shopping so much, probably will know this great shopping mall. This is one of the biggest shopping mall available in Bekasi. Located in one of Bekasi's infamous area, around Mall Metropolitan and some other mall, this will be a perfect destination who crave for shopping.
