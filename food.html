Telur Gabus

Telur Gabus is a traditional food from Bekasi / Betawi. It's made out of flour, egg, butter, and most of the time, cheese. It's fried, and will have a crunchy texture due to the nature of any fried food. The taste is actually vary, depending on which variant we buy. Usually, we can get it in sweet variant and cheese variant.

Kue Dongkal

Kue Dongkal is a traditional food from Bekasi / Betawi. It's a legendary food since it's been here since a long time ago. It's mostly made out of flour and coconut. The taste is similar to Kue Putu combined with Kue Lupis. It's sweet because of the brown sugar added as a fla of some sort.
